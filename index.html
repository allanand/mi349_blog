Rough Week

By Andrew Allan

Saturday, October 15th, 2022

Post Big House

Hello world! After returning from Big House I had a lot of homework to make up. This resulted in me doing homework for nearly 8 hours every day! I got little to no sleep during this period resulting in me offloading my sleep Thursday only putting me more behind on work.

 

Hurt My Finger

Thursday morning I woke up and my ring finger hurt. The night before I played some games online with friends for a few hours, but it was nothing really demanding, so I have no idea why it may have hurt. As a result of this happening, I had to cancel plans to play games with my Friends in person Thursday night. Some of my friends thought I was making excuses which just made it even more frustrating.

 

Other Annoying Stuff

  • I Found out I have to redo a class project
  • I burned soup all over my stove
  • I had to miss the MSU football game to finish an assignment
  • My Amazon order did not arrive on time

 

The Upside

While this week did suck, I at least had a good time Wednesday with my friends online and Friday at Smash Club. For those that do not know, Fridays is when MSU Smash Ultimate club hosts their weekly event. Since I am the Vice President of the club, I have a lot of responsibilities and must be there. However, I always have a fun time organizing the event, hanging out with all of my friends, and competing. While I did not compete too well because I could not play all week, I still learned a lot and had a good time overall. Afterwards, we also ended up going to Blaze pizza and I had the most tasty meat lovers pizza with bbq sauce!

 

Final Thoughts

From now on, I am making sure I plan better so I don't have so much homework. I did all of my homework until Monday, but that was unfortunately not enough.

 

 

Big House 10 Experience

By Andrew Allan

Sunday, October 9th, 2022

Bracket

Hello world! I recently got back from Big House 10! For those who don't know what that is, it's pretty much a large gaming tournament at the Cobo Center Detroit for the game Super Smash Brother Ultimate. I had a great time and improved a lot. I went 2-2 in singles almost getting out of round 1 of pools, or the first bracket, but I unfortunately lost my final match to get out. The person I was playing had their boyfriend giving them advice during the set which was really frustrating and likely illegal, but it's already too late to say something.

 

Vendors

There were a lot of vendors at the event, so I ended ended up spending a lot of money. My biggest buy was a brand new custom controller. It matches with the theme of the event being yellow and blue and looks so dam cool! The only unfortuate part is that they are U of M colors, however, I think the event is distinct enough where most people would not consider me a traitor of MSU for using the contoller. I also bought some plushies, a beanie, a mask, and some pins for my event lanyard.

 

Other Keynotes

  • I won a money match against my friend
  • I ran a Smash Melee bracket as an organizer (with no experience playing the game)
  • I played the 2nd best player in the US of my character (Inkling) and did well
  • I had a really fun adventure with my friend who lost his car in the MGM garage for 45 minutes

 

Final Thoughts

Overall, I had a great time and am looking forward to going again next year! If you have never been to a Smash Tournament before, you should totally give shot because it can be a lot of fun! Even if you are not good at the game, there are a lot of other activities you can do while there like play the arcade machines, or enter fun side brackets for other Nintendo games.
