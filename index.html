Big House 10 Experience

By Andrew Allan Monday October 10th, 2022

Bracket

Hello world! I recently got back from Big House 10! For those who don't know what that is, it's pretty much a large gaming tournament in Detroit for the game Super Smash Brother Ultimate. I had a great time and improved a lot. I went 2-2 in singles almost getting out of round 1 of pools, or the first bracket, but I unfortunately lost my final match to get out. The person I was playing had their boyfriend giving them advice during the set which was really frustrating and likely illegal, but it's already too late to say something.

Vendors

There were a lot of vendors at the event, so I ended ended up spending a lot of money. My biggest buy was a brand new custom controller. It matches with the theme of the event being yellow and blue and looks so dam cool! The only unfortuate part is that they are U of M colors, however, I think the event is distinct enough where most people would not consider me a traitor of MSU for using the contoller. I also bought some plushies, a beanie, a mask, and some pins for my event lanyard.

Keynotes

Some other keynotes were that...
  • I won a money match against my friend
  • I ran a Smash Melee bracket as an organizer (with no experience playing the game)
  • I played the 2nd best player in the US of my character (Inkling) and did well
  • I had a really fun adventure with my friend who lost his car in the MGM garage for 45 minutes

    • Final Thoughts

    Overwall, I had a great time and am looking forward to going again next year! If you have never been to a Smash Tournament before, you should totally give shot becasue it can be a lot of fun! Even if you are not good at the game, there are a lot of other activities you can do while there like play the aracde machines, or enter fun side brackets for other Nintendo games.
